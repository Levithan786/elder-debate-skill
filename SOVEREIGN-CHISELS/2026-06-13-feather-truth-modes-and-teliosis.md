## Decree

Feathers are now first-class extensions of the debate organism. A feather is not decoration. A feather is a lightweight but accountable truth-and-purpose membrane.

Each feather must carry:

1. A stable name.
2. A distinct mode of truth.
3. A distinct mode of teliosis.
4. A truth question.
5. An explicit teliosis.
6. Evidence requirements.
7. An output contract.
8. Risk posture and dependencies where needed.
9. Proof-gate verification before ratification.

## Modes of Truth

- **Witness:** truth of what was directly observed or supplied.
- **Ledger:** truth of permanent provenance, commits, issues, chisels, and records.
- **Adversarial:** truth discovered by falsification, counterexample, and veto.
- **Operational:** truth proven by execution, implementation, importability, tests, or build path.
- **Coherence:** truth of symbolic and conceptual fit without hiding gaps.
- **Sovereign:** truth of alignment with Jan/Sulimania telosis and command integrity.
- **Agora:** truth that survives plural debate and negotiated clause formation.
- **Horizon:** truth of forecasted consequence, rollback condition, and second-order effect.

## Modes of Teliosis

- **Record:** preserve the immediate given.
- **Remember:** preserve trace and memory.
- **Protect:** block corrupt or ungrounded extension.
- **Build:** convert doctrine into artifact.
- **Nourish:** improve beauty, language, and symbolic clarity without weakening proof.
- **Align:** keep every limb obedient to the sovereign purpose layer.
- **Negotiate:** turn plural positions into accepted and rejected clauses.
- **Anticipate:** see future consequences before growth.
